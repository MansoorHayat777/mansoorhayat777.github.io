About

I am an NSERC-funded Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Manitoba, Section of Neurosurgery, working at the intersection of medical imaging AI, physiological signal processing, and clinical decision support. My research focuses on building production-grade deep learning systems that are interpretable, uncertainty-aware, and deployable in real clinical environments — 24/7 ICU pipelines included.

I hold a PhD in Data Science with Distinction from Chulalongkorn University, an MSc and BSc in Electrical Engineering, and an MBA in Accounting and Finance. I am a licensed Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) with Engineers Geoscientists Manitoba and am pursuing interprovincial transfer to EGBC (British Columbia).

I have authored 27 peer-reviewed publications across IEEE Access, Frontiers in Physiology, Sensors, Bioengineering, and IEEE conference proceedings, and received the Best Paper Award at IEEE TENCON 2023. I serve as Area Chair for IJCNN 2025 and review for IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging, IEEE Access, and Nature Scientific Reports. Open to academic and industry opportunities in Vancouver, Montreal, Toronto, and the San Francisco Bay Area.
