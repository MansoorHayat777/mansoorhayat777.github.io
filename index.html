About

I am an NSERC-funded Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Manitoba, Section of Neurosurgery (Rady Faculty of Health Sciences), working at the intersection of medical imaging AI, physiological signal processing, and clinical decision support. My research focuses on building production-grade deep learning systems that are interpretable, uncertainty-aware, and deployable in real clinical environments.

I hold a PhD in Data Science with Distinction from Chulalongkorn University, an MSc and BSc in Electrical Engineering, and an MBA in Accounting and Finance. I am a licensed P.Eng. with Engineers Geoscientists Manitoba (APEGM) and serve as Area Chair for IJCNN 2025. I review for IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging, IEEE Access, Scientific Reports, and 60+ other journals, with 573+ completed peer reviews.

Open to academic positions (Assistant Professor, Research Associate) in Vancouver, Montreal, Toronto, Saskatoon, and the San Francisco Bay Area.
